### (1) Query Coordinator와 병렬 서버 프로세스

```sql
select /*+ ordered use_hash(d) full(d) full(e) nonparallel(d) parallel(e 4) */
       count(*), min(sal), max(sal), avg(sal), sum(sal)
from   dept d, emp e
where  d.loc = 'CHICAGO'
and    e.deptno = d.deptno;
```
